FAQS : Your questions answered

  • What is TechVerse 2024?

    TechVerse 2024 is an annual hackathon hosted by Anugya Enterprises at the prestigious IIT Delhi. This year’s event challenges participants to leverage cutting-edge technologies in Drones and Robotics, AI/ML, and Open Source. It's a platform for innovation where technology enthusiasts from various backgrounds come together to shape the future.

  • Who can participate in TechVerse 2024?

    TechVerse 2024 welcomes individuals and teams from across the globe. Whether you are a student, working professional, freelancer, or part of a startup, if you have a drive for technological innovation and a desire to make an impact, this hackathon is for you.

  • How will the hackathon be conducted?

    TechVerse 2024 follows a structured format starting with online registrations and idea submissions. Participants whose ideas are shortlisted will move on to develop their prototypes. The event culminates in a dynamic showcase at IIT Delhi, where participants present their solutions to a panel of experts from the tech industry.

  • Is there any participation fee?

    No, there is no participation fee for TechVerse 2024. We are committed to fostering a community of innovators and keeping the event accessible to all interested participants.

  • How can I register and participate?

    Registration for TechVerse 2024 is straightforward. Visit our registration page on the TechVerse website during the registration window, fill out the necessary details, and submit your initial idea. You can form teams of up to five members, and if you're looking for a team, you can join our discord community to connect you with other participants.

  • When will the winners be announced?

    The winners of TechVerse 2024 will be announced during the closing ceremony at IIT Delhi. Keep an eye on our website and official social media channels for updates and announcements.
